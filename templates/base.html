the party

The Afro Banana Republic is a community type of party. A concept used in festivals, which is achieved by creating an environment contrary to its context.

People thrown in this environment will quickly create a community; hence an amazing experience will be delivered upon them.

food & drinks

Since the community is addictive you will spend a great amount of time there. You will be spending a lot of time consuming alcoholic liquids available from the bar our community leaders have built. Burgers will be available.

Free tea for everyone.

location

The party is open-air at a deserted restaurant at Ayious Trimithias, Nicosia.

For directions click on the map or contact us: 96440420.
Follow the bananas!

community leaders

Cotsios o Pikatillis The best Afrobeat from the heart of the deepest African forests. Funk from the rarest collections in the world handpicked by James Brown himself.

Dr. Cusack Disco from the most underground disco clubs of the 80's selected carefully in a meeting within a giant Disco ball. Techno from the darkest clubs of Berlin and Detroit.

Koulla P. Katsikoronou Rhythms from the heart of the Balkan mountains chosen with Kusturitsa and Bregovic themselves from a chicken farm. Electro swing in a meeting from the last visit of Parov Stellar at his house.

Apostolos Rock 'n' Roll stolen from the golden sink of the King. Cutting and splicing DJ Afrika Bambaataa's tape sections, backspinning records and music editing live on his own computer.

Static Noise Live and recorded visuals from the everyday life of community members sprinkled with the colours of the real rainbow.
